MemoryCube↗

React · TypeScript · Three.js · Supabase · Claude AI
Shipped
v0.1, shipped Feb 2026 for a competition deadline

An NYU Tisch ITP thesis project. Instead of a flat photo album or an endless camera roll, MemoryCube builds an interactive 3D cube where each face captures a different dimension of a moment: neighborhood, mood, food, time of day, people, and the in-between details. Upload photos to each face and the memory becomes something you can rotate, share, and revisit as a living 3D object, with an AI-generated story woven from what you uploaded.
